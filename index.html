About Us

Our founders, Rain, Jacob, and Ashley, are childhood friends who were inlove with crafted bracelets back in their younger years. When they met again back in summer of 2021, they thought of starting a business. They remembered they were inlove with bracelets and decided to renew this interest by crafting their own brancelet brand. That is how Greystone started. The pieces we make are collection of love and interest for quality made bracelets.
